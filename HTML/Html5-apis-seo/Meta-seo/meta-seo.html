Meta Tags & SEO in HTML

What Are Meta Tags?

Meta tags are snippets of text that describe a page's content. They don't appear on the page itself but in the <head> section.

Common Meta Tags

  • Description: A brief description of the page.
  • Keywords: Relevant keywords for search engines.
  • Author: The creator of the webpage.
  • Robots: Instructions for search engine crawlers.

Why SEO Matters

Search Engine Optimization (SEO) helps your webpage appear higher in search results. Using meta tags correctly is one of the first steps in making your page more discoverable.
